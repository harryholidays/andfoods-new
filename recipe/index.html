Plant-Based Chocolate Éclairs

Decadent choux pastry shells filled with a rich Andfoods vanilla pastry cream and finished with a glossy dark chocolate glaze. Built to impress, without a drop of dairy.

This recipe is built around our barista-ready creamy base, delivering richness, mouthfeel and a clean finish with no dairy. Available for food service, manufacturing and NPD teams.

Ingredients

  • 500ml Andfoods barista-ready creamy base
  • 140ml aquafaba (chickpea brine), room temp
  • 80ml Andfoods barista-ready creamy base (for glaze)
  • 1½ tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 180g dark chocolate (70% cocoa), finely chopped

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C / 180°C fan. Line two baking trays with baking paper and lightly brush them with water, steam helps lift the pastry.
  2. Combine the water, plant-based butter, sugar and salt in a saucepan. Bring to a rolling boil until the butter is completely melted.
  3. Remove from heat. Add all the sifted flour at once and beat vigorously with a wooden spoon until a smooth ball forms and pulls away cleanly from the sides of the pan.
  4. With the mixer running, gradually add the aquafaba 2 tablespoons at a time until the dough is smooth, glossy and drops from a spoon in a slow V-shape ribbon.
  5. In a bowl, whisk together the sugar, cornflour and salt. Pour in a quarter of the warm cream and whisk to a smooth paste. Return the paste to the pan with the remaining cream.
  6. Warm the Andfoods creamy base in a saucepan over medium heat until just steaming. Do not let it boil.
  7. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, for 4–5 minutes until very thick and glossy. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract.
  8. Transfer to a bowl and press cling film directly onto the surface to prevent a skin forming. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour until completely cold.
  9. Whip the chilled pastry cream until smooth, then transfer to a piping bag with a small plain tip. Pierce three small holes along the base of each éclair and pipe in the cream until you feel light resistance.
  10. For the glaze, heat the Andfoods creamy base until just simmering. Pour over the chopped chocolate and maple syrup. Let stand for 1 minute, then stir until smooth and glossy.
  11. Dip the top of each filled éclair into the warm glaze. Let any excess drip off, then set on a rack for 15 minutes until the glaze firms. Serve at room temperature. Best enjoyed the same day.
