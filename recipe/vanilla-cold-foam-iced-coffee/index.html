Vanilla Cold Foam Iced Coffee

Smooth cold brew topped with a light, velvety cold foam.

Ingredients

Base

  • 150 ml cold brew coffee
  • 10 ml vanilla syrup
  • Ice

Cold Foam

  • 60 ml ANDWHIP
  • 20 ml milk of choice
  • 10 ml vanilla syrup

Method

  1. Fill a cup with ice and add cold brew and syrup.
  2. Froth cold foam ingredients until thick and silky.
  3. Gently layer foam over the coffee.
  4. Finish with a light dusting of coffee powder.
