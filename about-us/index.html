About Us

From Pulses to Performance

The next generation of cream: engineered for function and taste.

Harnessing the Power of Pulses

We use functional plant proteins derived from pulses, like black mung bean, to create the structure and stability needed for modern food applications.

The result is creamy products that combine familiar food origins with reliable performance in professional kitchens.

Built on New Zealand Dairy Expertise

We draw on New Zealand's globally recognised expertise in dairy and food production to develop products that meet the performance standards chefs expect.

This foundation helps ensure consistent quality, reliability, and ease of use in demanding kitchen environments.

Designed Through Innovation

Our team combines food science, product development, and manufacturing expertise to create ingredients that work in real foodservice settings.

Working with leading research partners, we focus on practical innovation, developing products that are scalable, versatile, and built for everyday use.

Together, these foundations enable Andfoods to develop next-generation products designed for modern foodservice.

Our Team

Alex Devereux
CEO

Starting his career at Fonterra then moving into roles in the technology and investment sectors, Alex always wanted to make a significant contribution in the food industry. At Andfoods, he channels his broad experience into pioneering sustainable, plant-based dairy alternatives alongside a talented team of New Zealand's top food scientists, crafting foodservice and ingredient formats that set new standards in the plant-based segment.
